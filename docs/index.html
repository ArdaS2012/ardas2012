About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio website! I am a student of robotics, cognition, intelligence at the TU Munich. My focus is on machine learning and data processing. I love to develop strategies and algorithms for any kind of problems. I am also very curious and like to tackle challenges with enthusiasm. This website is meant to present a part of my projects and to underline my skills in these areas. As this website is a brief outline of myself, you are more than welcome to download my curriculum vitae for more information or contact me at the following email address: arda.sener.fb@hotmail.de.

Experience

Audi AG

Masterand in the Field of KI

The topic of my master’s thesis is: "Detection of driving factors for KPI calculation in GoldenAlgo dataset." In this project, I am researching an approach that can find bad KPI values in vehicle perception data.

Fraunhofer IIS EZRT

Research Assistant for the Conception of Neural Networks

I started working on a project with the title: "Design of Deep Neural Networks for Input Data from Multimodal Imaging Sensor Systems to Determine the Value of Printed Circuit Boards in Recycling" as my bachelor's thesis. After finishing my thesis I was extended for this project. I learned how to handle multimodal data which in this case was x-ray and rgb data. A complete pipeline of pre-processing, training, evaluation and visualization was built. The code was programmed in Python and various libraries for Deep Learning were used such as Tensorflow, PyTorch, OpenCV etc.

Siemens AG

Working student for the CATS-Flexible System

This position was primarily concerned with maintaining general programming activities for the CATS-Flexible software. I was also tasked with fixing the internal git servers and bugs. I worked a lot with C# and other unit tests in this position.

University of Utah

Internship Abroad in the Field of Nanotechnology

The project of this internship was to build a very thin camera. My main task was mainly to program the camera (installation on Raspberry Pi). The main innovation here was that the thin lens was made of meta materials,which had to be attached to the camera. I also provided support for other, projects such as the 3D-Printing in resin with a lens based on metamaterials.
